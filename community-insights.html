Overview

Executive Summary

10X's Community is an AI and automation learning platform hosting 315 members with a robust curriculum of 117 lessons across 5+ sprints and 94 past live events. While the content infrastructure is strong, the community faces a significant engagement crisis: 63.5% of members have never posted or commented, per-post engagement averages just 0.19 likes, and 74% of members have zero gamification points. Content creation is heavily admin-driven, with "10x Support" authoring nearly half of recent posts. The community has no upcoming events scheduled, and 52% of its 27 spaces sit empty. Immediate action is needed to activate the silent majority, diversify content creation, and revitalize the event pipeline.

Action Plan

Recommendations

Action

Launch Weekly Engagement Challenges

Create themed weekly challenges (e.g., "Share your AI automation win") with gamification point rewards. This gives silent members a low-friction reason to participate.

Action

Consolidate Empty Spaces

Archive or merge the 14 empty spaces into active ones. A focused set of 10-12 spaces will concentrate activity, making each space feel alive and active.

Action

Member Spotlight Program

Feature one community member weekly with their learning journey and project. This shifts the content dynamic from admin-broadcast to community-driven storytelling.

Action

Schedule Upcoming Events Immediately

The event pipeline is empty. Schedule at least 4-6 weeks of upcoming events to signal active community life. Visibility of future events drives retention and return visits.

Action

Onboarding Automation for Silent Members

Implement automated nudges for the 63.5% who have never engaged: welcome DMs, "introduce yourself" prompts, first-post incentives, and 7-day drip sequences.

Action

Revamp Gamification System

Tie points to meaningful actions: posting (10 pts), commenting (5 pts), completing a sprint (50 pts), attending live events (15 pts). Add visible badges and milestone rewards.

Action

Introduce "Post of the Week" Feature

Highlight the best community post each week with a pinned announcement and bonus gamification points. This incentivizes quality contributions, gives recognition to active members, and provides social proof that the community values member-generated content over admin broadcasts. Consider adding a voting mechanism to increase comment engagement.
